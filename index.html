SERVICES

ENHANCES LIFE OF THE VEHICLE
A qualified maintainer inspects the vehicle during the periodic servicing and undertakes necessary repair or replacement. Failing which, it may lead to cascading effect and turn the associated components defective. Consequently, the overall health of the vehicle increases considerably.

IMPROVES ON-ROAD PERFORMANCE
The performance of the car on the road deteriorates over the period due to the usage and wear and tear of components. The repair, refurbishment, and replacement during the regular car service process will be instrumental in restoring the performance of the car. Thus, you can enjoy the drive as in a new car, when you pursue regular car servicing without fail.





SAFETY ASPECTS
Imagine a condition wherein you applied brakes suddenly and the tyre bursts. It can be a major accident, which may turn fatal. One of the main reasons for tyre bursts is the wearing out of plies. Although an individual can inspect it, many times we miss out on checking the tyres. The technical team at the maintenance centre will examine every component and do the needful to prevent any mishaps or problems. This is a point of paramount importance among the benefits of regular car servicing.


ENSURE FUEL-EFFICIENCY
The wear and tear of engine components and accessories and other similar issues can affect the mileage of the car. In turn, your car will not have the fuel efficiency it had during the initial days. Regular periodic servicing will be beneficial in reinstating the fuel efficiency of the car. And saving you money in this era of skyrocketing fuel prices.


ENGINE HEALTH MONITORING
Regular servicing is vital for engine health monitoring. The technicians will not only visually inspect the engine but also will undertake a functional inspection to ascertain the condition. It benefits in improving the engine performance optimally.

FUNCTIONING OF ELECTRICAL COMPONENTS AND INDICATORS
You might have seen the frequent failure of warning lamps, irregularity in indicator functioning etcetera as you use the vehicle for a prolonged period, without maintenance. This may turn troublesome during the drive, especially during long drives. Imagine you get an engine low oil warning that may be only due to electric malfunction. The warning will put you in panic and affect the enjoyment of the trip. Regular car servicing will save you from this.
